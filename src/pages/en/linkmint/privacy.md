This policy describes how user information is handled in "LinkMint" (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

**The App does not collect or transmit any user information.**

- **Data you enter or generate**: Text and URLs you enter, links you generate, history, and favorites are stored **only on your device (on-device)**. They are never sent to the developer's or any third party's servers.
- **Network communication**: The App itself does not communicate with any server. When you perform an action to open a link, the URL is passed—as your action—to the device's default browser (Safari, etc.) or the target app.
- **Accounts**: The App does not require account registration.
- The App **shows no ads** and performs none of the following: analytics, advertising-identifier collection, tracking, or crash-data collection.

## 2. Photo Library

Only when you perform an action to save a QR code as an image does the App **write** an image to your photo library, as your action. The App does not read from your photo library.

## 3. Navigation to External Sites

Links in the settings screen—"Support (Buy Me a Coffee)," "Terms of Use," and "Privacy Policy"—open external sites in the device's default browser. Handling of information at the destination follows each site's own policy.

## 4. Provision to Third Parties

Because the App does not collect user information, there is no information to provide to third parties.

## 5. Managing and Deleting Data

- History and favorites can be deleted individually at any time within the App.
- Deleting the App from your device erases all data stored on the device.

## 6. Revisions

This policy may be revised without prior notice. Revised content takes effect once posted on this page.

## 7. Contact

For questions about this policy, please use the [contact form](/en/linkmint/contact).
