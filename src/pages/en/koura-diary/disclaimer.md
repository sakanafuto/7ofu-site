Thank you for using "Koura Diary" (the "App"). This is the disclaimer regarding the health and nutrition information shown in the App.

## 1. Not Professional Advice

Information shown by the App (dietary-balance assessment, nutrition guides for food, carapace body-condition guides, etc.) is a **general reference guide for care** and is **not veterinary care, medical practice, or professional nutritional guidance**.

- Do not use it as a basis for decisions such as diagnosis, treatment, or medication.
- If you have concerns about your tortoise's health, please consult a **veterinarian who handles reptiles**.

## 2. Guides Vary by Species and Individual

Nutrition data and body-condition indices (such as the Jackson ratio) are based on **conservative general values** assuming species such as Mediterranean tortoises. What is appropriate varies greatly by **species, individual, age, growth stage, and season**. The display is not a definitive assessment.

## 3. Accuracy of Data

Nutrition and edibility data are curated based on general husbandry information, but their **completeness, currency, and accuracy are not guaranteed**. Foods whose nutrition is not published, such as commercial pellets, are treated as "unknown."

## 4. Self-Responsibility

Use the information in the App at your own discretion and responsibility. The developer bears no liability for any damages arising from use of, or inability to use, the App.

This disclaimer may be revised without prior notice. Revised content takes effect once posted on this page.
