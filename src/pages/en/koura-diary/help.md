The basics of using Koura Diary.

## Getting Started

- The app is ready to use the moment you open it (no sign-in required; it starts anonymously).
- First, register the tortoise (animal) you keep. You can enter its name, species, sex, adoption date, and more, and set a profile photo.
- If you keep several, you can register as many as you like and switch between them at the top of the screen.

## Daily Records

You can record easily from the day's card.

- Weight / carapace length (g / cm)
- Temperature / humidity (habitat)
- Food (what you fed; a nutrition-balance guide is shown)
- Excretion (yes / no)
- Carapace photo (to record growth)
- Notes (free text)

Using "carry over the previous record" lets you quickly fill in items that don't change.

## Looking Back

- Charts … See trends in weight and carapace length, and the distribution of food.
- Calendar … Check things like excretion month by month.
- Album … Line up carapace photos chronologically to look back on growth. Tap a photo to enlarge and save it.

## Sharing with Family (Households)

- Create a "household" to share records with family.
- Once shared, members of the same household can **view and edit** records, photos, and animal profiles.
- You can leave a household at any time. Choose whom to share with at your own discretion (see the [Terms of Use](/en/koura-diary/terms)).

## Reminders

- To help you not forget care, you can set notification reminders (local notifications on the device). Adjust them from Settings.

## Backup and Restore

- From "Settings → Data and Backup," you can **back up (export) and restore (import)** all data, including photos.
- When you change devices, you can restore from a backup to carry everything over.

## Account / Leaving

- With Apple / Google sign-in, you can link an account used for multi-device sync and household sharing (optional).
- You can leave (delete your account) from Settings. Linked data is deleted as well.

## FAQ

**Do I need to sign in?**
No. You can use all features without signing in. Linking is only needed when you want to sync or share.

**How much should I trust the health / nutrition display?**
It's a general guide. It is not a diagnosis, so if you are worried, consult a veterinarian who handles reptiles (see the [Disclaimer](/en/koura-diary/disclaimer)).

**Can I carry data over when I change devices?**
Yes. You can carry it over via Apple / Google sync, or via backup → restore.

**Are there ads?**
No. There is no tracking, either (see the [Privacy Policy](/en/koura-diary/privacy)).

## Contact

If something isn't working or you have a request, please contact us from "Settings → Contact" in the app.
